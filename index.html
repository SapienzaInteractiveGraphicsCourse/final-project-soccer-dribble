🎮 Basic Controls

E Switch Player

T Sliding tackle

🚀 The Boost

Hold SPACEBAR

Activates a powerful sprint to push the ball forward and launch a lightning-fast counterattack.

  • Rapidly consumes the Boost Bar (Yellow).
  • Recharge the boost by running over the Glowing Pads on the pitch.

💎 Bonuses & Power-Ups

Collect diamonds on the pitch. The bonus activates only on the next shot (Right Click). If you pass the ball or switch players, the bonus disappears!

🔥 SUPER SHOT

Power x2.5. The ball leaves a trail of fire and travels straight at devastating speed.

⚡ ELECTRIC SHOT

Power x2.5. The ball follows an unpredictable zig-zag trajectory for the goalkeeper.
